Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Introduction

RealLifeHQ ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our iOS application.

Your privacy is paramount to us. All data created and stored within RealLifeHQ remains exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal data with third parties or our servers. Sensitive information like passwords is protected using iOS Keychain, Apple's most secure storage system.

Information We Collect

RealLifeHQ stores the following types of data locally on your device using iOS's secure local storage:

Calendar Events

  • Event titles, dates, times, and notes
  • Completion status
  • Reminder settings

Habit Tracking Data

  • Habit names, icons, and colors
  • Completion dates and streaks
  • Frequency and schedule settings

Journal Entries

  • Entry content and dates
  • Mood tracking information
  • Tags for categorization

Budget & Financial Information

  • Budget setup and income information
  • Expense transactions and categories
  • Recurring expense schedules
  • Category limits and spending data

Recipe Information

  • Recipe names, ingredients, and instructions
  • Cooking times and servings
  • Meal plans and shopping lists
  • Favorite recipe selections

Vault Items (Secure Storage)

  • Titles and categories
  • Usernames and website URLs
  • Passwords (stored securely in iOS Keychain)
  • Secure notes (stored securely in iOS Keychain)
  • Attached photos (stored as encrypted data)

App Settings & Preferences

  • Theme preferences
  • Notification settings
  • Biometric authentication preferences
  • Dashboard widget configurations

Data NOT Collected

We want to be crystal clear about what we DO NOT collect:

  • No Personal Identifiers: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
  • No Location Data: We do not access or collect your location information
  • No Analytics: We do not use analytics services to track your app usage
  • No Advertising Data: We do not collect data for advertising purposes
  • No Device Information: We do not collect information about your device beyond what iOS provides for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

All data you create within RealLifeHQ is:

  1. Stored Locally: All information is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure local storage mechanisms
  2. User-Controlled: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time
  3. Never Transmitted: Your data is never sent to external servers, cloud services, or third parties
  4. Not Shared: We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with anyone

Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access Your Data: All your data is accessible within the app at any time
  • Delete Your Data: Delete any items individually, use 'Clear All Data' in Settings, or uninstall the app to remove all associated data
  • Export Your Data: Use the 'Export Data' feature in Settings to create a backup of your information
  • Control Permissions: Manage notifications, photos, and biometrics through iOS Settings or in-app Settings

Third-Party Services

RealLifeHQ does NOT integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage providers
  • Social media platforms
  • Payment processors (other than Apple's in-app purchase system)

The app uses only standard iOS frameworks:

  • iOS Keychain (for secure password storage)
  • Local Notifications (data remains on device)
  • Photo Library (your choice)
  • Biometric Authentication (processed by iOS)
  • StoreKit (for subscriptions, managed by Apple)

Children's Privacy

RealLifeHQ is suitable for users of all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information from users, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Parents can allow children to use the app with confidence that no data is being collected or transmitted.
