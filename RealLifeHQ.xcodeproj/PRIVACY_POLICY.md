# Privacy Policy for RealLifeHQ

**Last Updated: January 25, 2026**

## Introduction

RealLifeHQ ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our iOS application.

**Your privacy is paramount to us. All data created and stored within RealLifeHQ remains exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any of your personal data with third parties or our servers.**

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

RealLifeHQ stores the following types of data locally on your device using iOS's secure local storage (UserDefaults and device storage):

#### 1. **Calendar Events**
- Event titles, dates, times, and notes
- Completion status
- Reminder settings

#### 2. **Habit Tracking Data**
- Habit names, icons, and colors
- Completion dates and streaks
- Frequency and schedule settings

#### 3. **Journal Entries**
- Entry content and dates
- Mood tracking information
- Tags for categorization

#### 4. **Budget & Financial Information**
- Budget setup and income information
- Expense transactions and categories
- Recurring expense schedules
- Category limits and spending data

#### 5. **Recipe Information**
- Recipe names, ingredients, and instructions
- Cooking times and servings
- Meal plans and shopping lists
- Favorite recipe selections

#### 6. **Vault Items (Secure Storage)**
- Titles and categories
- Usernames and passwords (stored locally)
- Website URLs and notes
- Attached photos (stored as encrypted data)

#### 7. **App Settings & Preferences**
- Theme preferences
- Notification settings
- Biometric authentication preferences
- Dashboard widget configurations

### Data NOT Collected

- **No Personal Identifiers**: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.
- **No Location Data**: We do not access or collect your location information.
- **No Analytics**: We do not use analytics services to track your app usage.
- **No Advertising Data**: We do not collect data for advertising purposes.
- **No Device Information**: We do not collect information about your device beyond what iOS provides for app functionality.

## How We Use Your Information

All data you create within RealLifeHQ is:

1. **Stored Locally**: All information is stored exclusively on your device using iOS's secure local storage mechanisms
2. **User-Controlled**: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time
3. **Never Transmitted**: Your data is never sent to external servers, cloud services, or third parties
4. **Not Shared**: We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with anyone

### Specific Feature Uses:

#### Notifications
- Local notifications are scheduled on your device to remind you of upcoming events
- Notification content (event titles and notes) remains on your device
- Notification permissions are optional and can be managed in iOS Settings

#### Biometric Authentication (Face ID/Touch ID)
- Used solely to unlock the secure Vault feature
- Biometric data is processed entirely by iOS and never accessed by the app
- Authentication is optional and can be disabled at any time

#### Photos
- The app can access your photo library only when you choose to attach photos to Vault items
- Selected photos are stored locally within the app's secure storage
- No photos are uploaded or transmitted externally

## Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your information:

### Local Storage Security
- All data is stored using iOS's secure storage mechanisms
- Vault passwords and sensitive information remain on your device
- Attached photos are stored as encrypted data within the app

### Biometric Protection
- Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection for the Vault feature
- Leverages iOS's secure biometric authentication system
- Adds an additional layer of security for sensitive information

### iOS Sandbox
- The app operates within iOS's secure app sandbox
- Data is isolated from other apps and protected by iOS security features

## Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

### Access Your Data
- All your data is accessible within the app at any time

### Delete Your Data
- **Individual Items**: Delete any calendar events, habits, journal entries, recipes, vault items, or other data within the app
- **Clear All Data**: Use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings to remove all app data from your device
- **Uninstall App**: Deleting the app from your device removes all associated data

### Export Your Data
- Use the "Export Data" feature in Settings to create a backup of your information
- Exported data is in a standard format for your records

### Control Permissions
- **Notifications**: Enable or disable in iOS Settings → Notifications → RealLifeHQ
- **Photos**: Manage photo library access in iOS Settings → RealLifeHQ → Photos
- **Biometrics**: Enable or disable Face ID/Touch ID in app Settings

## Third-Party Services

RealLifeHQ does NOT integrate with any third-party services, including:
- Analytics platforms
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage providers
- Social media platforms
- Payment processors (other than Apple's in-app purchase system, if applicable)

### Apple Services
The app uses only the following standard iOS frameworks:
- **Local Notifications**: For event reminders (data remains on device)
- **Photo Library**: When you choose to attach photos (your choice)
- **Biometric Authentication**: For Vault security (processed by iOS)
- **App Store**: For app distribution and updates (managed by Apple)
