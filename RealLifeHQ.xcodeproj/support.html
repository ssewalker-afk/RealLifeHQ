üìñ Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I sync with Apple Calendar?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Integrations ‚Üí Apple Calendar Sync and enable the toggle. You'll be asked to grant calendar access permissions.
‚ùì How do I back up my data?
Your data is automatically backed up through iCloud if you have iCloud backup enabled for this app. You can also manually export your data from Settings ‚Üí Export Data.
‚ùì How do I set up recurring events?
When creating or editing an event, toggle 'Recurring Event' and choose your preferred frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly).
‚ùì What's the difference between Events and Habits?
Events are one-time or recurring appointments with specific dates and times. Habits are daily activities you want to track and build streaks for.
‚ùì How secure is the Vault?
Vault entries are encrypted using AES-256 encryption and can be protected with Face ID or Touch ID. Your notes are stored securely on your device.
‚ùì Can I use RealLifeHQ offline?
Yes! RealLifeHQ works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device. Internet is only needed for optional calendar sync features.
‚ùì How do I change my subscription?
You can manage your subscription through the App Store. Go to iPhone Settings ‚Üí [Your Name] ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí RealLifeHQ.
‚ùì How do I delete my data?
Go to Settings ‚Üí Data ‚Üí Clear All Data. This will permanently delete all your local data. If you have iCloud backup enabled, you may need to delete the backup separately.
‚ùì Why am I not receiving notifications?
Check that notifications are enabled in both iOS Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí RealLifeHQ and within the app at Settings ‚Üí Notifications. Also ensure Do Not Disturb is not blocking notifications.
‚ùì Can I use RealLifeHQ on multiple devices?
Yes! If you enable iCloud backup in iOS Settings, your data will sync across all your devices signed into the same Apple ID.

üîß Troubleshooting

Sync Issues

If calendar sync isn't working:

  • Check that you've granted calendar permissions in iOS Settings
  • Disable and re-enable the sync in RealLifeHQ Settings
  • Make sure you're signed into iCloud (for Apple Calendar)
  • Verify your internet connection (for Google Calendar)

Notification Problems

If you're not receiving notifications:

  • Enable notifications in iOS Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí RealLifeHQ
  • Check that Do Not Disturb is not active
  • Verify notification settings in RealLifeHQ Settings ‚Üí Notifications
  • Make sure you've set reminders on your events

App Performance

If the app is running slowly:

  • Restart the app by swiping up from the app switcher
  • Update to the latest version from the App Store
  • Restart your device
  • If issues persist, contact support

Data Recovery

If you've lost data:

  • Check if iCloud backup is enabled in iOS Settings
  • Try restoring from an iCloud backup
  • If you exported data previously, you can reimport it
  • Contact support if you need assistance with data recovery
